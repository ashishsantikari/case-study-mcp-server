# Legal Knowledge Base

## Solar Panel Installation Contracts
> **Markets:** Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich, Hamburg | **Devices:** Solar Panel | **Categories:** Contracts, Consumer-Rights

All Acme Energy solar panel installation contracts include a detailed Leistungsverzeichnis specifying the scope of work, materials, and expected energy yield. Customers have a 14-day Widerrufsrecht under German consumer protection law, during which they may cancel the contract without penalty. For installations in Berlin, additional requirements from the Berliner Solarpflicht must be explicitly incorporated into the contract terms. Our standard contract template has been reviewed by Fachanwälte für Vertragsrecht to ensure compliance with BGB §305 ff. on general terms and conditions.

## Battery Storage Warranty Terms
> **Markets:** Berlin, Munich | **Devices:** Battery-Storage, Inverter | **Categories:** Warranty, Liability

Acme Energy provides a 10-year manufacturer-backed warranty on all battery storage systems installed in the Berlin and Munich markets, covering capacity degradation below 70% of the original rated capacity. This extends beyond the statutory 2-year Gewährleistung under BGB §438 by an additional 8 years through our partner manufacturers. For Munich installations subject to the Bayerische Bauordnung, battery placement in listed buildings (denkmalgeschützte Gebäude) requires separate warranty provisions addressing structural integrity. The warranty does not cover damage caused by improper use, grid surges exceeding VDE thresholds, or unauthorized modifications.

## Inverter Warranty and Extended Coverage
> **Markets:** Frankfurt, Hamburg | **Devices:** Inverter | **Categories:** Warranty

Inverters installed in Frankfurt and Hamburg come with a standard 5-year manufacturer warranty, with an optional Acme Plus extension to 12 years. The Acme Plus program includes biannual remote diagnostics and priority on-site service within 48 hours of a reported fault. Hamburg's coastal climate necessitates additional corrosion-protection provisions, which are covered under the extended warranty but excluded from the standard terms. Customers must register their inverter warranty within 30 days of commissioning via the Acme Kundenportal for full coverage to apply.

## Liability for Electrical Installation Work
> **Markets:** Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich, Hamburg | **Devices:** Solar Panel, Inverter, Car-Charger | **Categories:** Liability

All electrical installation work is carried out by certified Elektrofachkräfte registered with the local Netzbetreiber and holding valid Installateurausweise. Acme Energy maintains a Betriebshaftpflichtversicherung with a coverage limit of EUR 10 million per claim, covering personal injury, property damage, and resulting financial loss. Installation crews follow VDE 0100 standards for low-voltage installations and VDE-AR-N 4105 for generator connections. In the event of damage caused by faulty installation, Acme Energy's liability is governed by BGB §823 and the Produkthaftungsgesetz, with claims processed through our dedicated Schadenmanagement team.

## Consumer Rights and Widerrufsrecht
> **Markets:** Berlin | **Devices:** Solar Panel, Battery-Storage | **Categories:** Consumer-Rights, Contracts

Berlin customers benefit from the full scope of German consumer protection under BGB §355, with a 14-day withdrawal right for all distance and off-premises contracts. The Widerrufsbelehrung is provided in duplicate at the time of contract signing, with a model withdrawal form included as required by EGBGB Art. 246a. For financed installations, the withdrawal from the purchase contract automatically terminates any linked Verbraucherdarlehensvertrag under BGB §358. Acme Energy's Berlin showroom in Kreuzberg allows customers to inspect equipment before signing, somewhat limiting the Fernabsatzrecht but strengthening informed consent.

## Data Protection in Smart Energy Devices
> **Markets:** Berlin, Hamburg | **Devices:** Solar Panel, Battery-Storage, Inverter, Car-Charger | **Categories:** Data-Protection

Smart energy devices deployed by Acme Energy collect operational data including generation metrics, consumption patterns, and grid feed-in statistics, all of which constitute personenbezogene Daten under the DSGVO when linked to a customer account. Data processing is governed by a comprehensive Auftragsverarbeitungsvertrag (AVV) between Acme Energy and each device manufacturer. In Hamburg, the Landesdatenschutzbeauftragter has issued specific guidance on smart meter data that requires explicit opt-in consent for consumption profiling beyond billing purposes. All telemetry data transmitted from customer premises is encrypted using TLS 1.3 and stored in ISO 27001-certified data centers within the EU.

## Grid Connection Requirements by Market
> **Markets:** Frankfurt, Munich | **Devices:** Solar Panel, Inverter | **Categories:** Contracts

Frankfurt installations must comply with the technical connection conditions (TAB) of the Mainova Netz GmbH, including mandatory Einspeisemanagement via a Funkrundsteuerempfänger for systems exceeding 10 kWp. Munich installations under the Stadtwerke München grid require compliance with TAB 2019 and the EEG 2023 feed-in tariff registration through the Marktstammdatenregister. Both markets mandate that an accredited Elektroinstallateur submit the Fertigmeldung to the Netzbetreiber before commissioning. Systems above 30 kWp require a separate NA-Schutz relay certified to VDE-AR-N 4105 for grid decoupling.

## Insurance Requirements for Renewable Installations
> **Markets:** Berlin, Hamburg | **Devices:** Solar Panel, Battery-Storage, Car-Charger | **Categories:** Contracts, Liability

Acme Energy requires all customers to maintain a Wohngebäudeversicherung that covers photovoltaic installations against fire, storm, hail, and water damage, with a recommended Deckungssumme matching the installation value. Battery storage systems in Hamburg's flood-prone areas (especially Wilhelmsburg and the HafenCity) require additional Elementarschadenversicherung covering flood and storm surge. For car chargers, customers are advised to extend their Haftpflichtversicherung to include charging infrastructure liability. Acme Energy offers an optional Versicherungspaket through a partnership with Allianz that bundles all required coverage at a preferential rate.

## Dispute Resolution and Schlichtung
> **Markets:** Frankfurt | **Devices:** Solar Panel, Inverter | **Categories:** Contracts, Consumer-Rights

Disputes arising from installation contracts in Frankfurt are primarily resolved through Acme Energy's internal Beschwerdemanagement procedure, which aims for resolution within 14 business days. If internal escalation fails, customers may access the Schlichtungsstelle Energie e.V., the recognized alternative dispute resolution body for the German energy sector under §111b EnWG. Frankfurt customers additionally benefit from the Industrie- und Handelskammer Frankfurt mediation service for commercial disputes. All Acme Energy contracts include a Schlichtungsklausel making mediation a prerequisite before either party may initiate legal proceedings before the Amtsgericht or Landgericht Frankfurt.
