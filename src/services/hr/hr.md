# HR Knowledge Base

## Remote Work Policy
> **Markets:** Berlin, Remote | **Categories:** Remote-Work, Policies

Enpal supports a hybrid working model for Berlin-based employees, requiring a minimum of two days per week in the office at our Kreuzberg headquarters. Fully remote employees with a Remote-Arbeitsvertrag may work from anywhere within Germany, provided they maintain a suitable home office environment that meets ergonomic standards per the Arbeitsstättenverordnung. Remote employees receive a one-time EUR 500 home office stipend for desk, chair, and monitor purchases, processed through our Expensify integration. Berlin-based employees switching to remote status must submit a formal Antrag auf Telearbeit through their team lead and HR business partner, with a transition period of four weeks.

## Vacation Leave and Regional Regulations
> **Markets:** Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich, Hamburg | **Categories:** Leave, Benefits

Employees are entitled to 30 vacation days per calendar year based on a 5-day work week, exceeding the statutory minimum of 20 days under the Bundesurlaubsgesetz. Unused vacation days may be carried forward until March 31 of the following year, with any remaining balance forfeited unless documented operational reasons prevented their use. Munich-based employees benefit from an additional day off for the Münchner Stadtgründungsfest on June 14, following local collective agreement practice. Hamburg employees may request floating public holidays for Hafengeburtstag trade participation, coordinated through departmental leave planners in Personio.

## Parental Leave and Familienleistungen
> **Markets:** Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich, Hamburg | **Categories:** Leave, Benefits, Policies

Enpal fully complies with the Bundeselterngeld- und Elternzeitgesetz (BEEG), offering up to 3 years of Elternzeit per child, with the first 12 months compensated through the state Elterngeld scheme. Beyond statutory requirements, Enpal tops up Elterngeld to 80% of net salary for the first 4 months of parental leave for employees with at least one year of tenure. Fathers and second parents are actively encouraged to take at least two months of leave through our Vaterzeit-Plus campaign, which includes a EUR 500 bonus upon return. Part-time work during Elternzeit is facilitated through flexible scheduling up to 32 hours per week, arranged with the employee's direct manager and documented in a Teilzeitvereinbarung.

## Health Insurance and Benefits Package
> **Markets:** Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich, Hamburg | **Categories:** Benefits, Well-Being

All Enpal employees are enrolled in the statutory Krankenversicherung (GKV) through automatic payroll deduction unless they opt out with proof of private insurance. Our company-subsidized betriebliche Krankenversicherung (bKV) package covers dental care up to EUR 600 per year, prescription glasses allowance of EUR 200 biennially, and access to premium specialist appointments through the Arzttermin-Service. Employees receive an annual Health Budget of EUR 300 through the Sodexo platform, redeemable for gym memberships, Urban Sports Club, massages, or wellness apps. Occupational health screenings are conducted annually by our partner Betriebsarzt in compliance with ArbMedVV and are mandatory for field technicians.

## Compensation and Bonus Structure
> **Markets:** Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich, Hamburg, Remote | **Categories:** Compensation, Benefits, Policies

Enpal's compensation framework is built on a market-competitive salary structure benchmarked against renewable energy sector data from Radford and StepStone. The annual performance bonus targets 10% of base salary for individual contributors and 15% for managers, with a multiplier based on company OKR achievement (0.5x–2.0x range) and individual performance rating. All permanent employees participate in the Enpal Virtual Stock Option Program (VSOP), vesting over a four-year period with a one-year cliff and monthly vesting thereafter. Frankfurt and Munich-based roles carry a Standortzuschlag of 5% on base salary to reflect higher local cost of living indices.

## Professional Development Budget
> **Markets:** Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich, Hamburg, Remote | **Categories:** Development, Benefits

Each employee receives an annual learning budget of EUR 1,500 for professional development, accessible through our Learnship platform integration. Eligible expenses include conference tickets, online courses (Coursera, Udemy, LinkedIn Learning), professional certifications (PMP, Scrum Master, AWS), and Fachbücher. Team leads may authorize additional budget for role-critical certifications beyond the EUR 1,500 cap, with approval routed through the finance department. Employees are entitled to five paid Bildungsurlaub days per year under the respective state laws, with Berlin and Hamburg recognizing this right under their Bildungsurlaubsgesetze for both professional and civic education.

## Onboarding Process for New Hires
> **Markets:** Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich, Hamburg, Remote | **Categories:** Onboarding, Policies

The Enpal onboarding journey begins with a Pre-Boarding phase two weeks before the start date, during which new hires receive their equipment (MacBook Pro, peripherals), access credentials, and a personalized Onboarding Map via Notion. Day one features a Welcome Morning at our Berlin office (or virtually for remote employees) covering company mission, organizational structure, and compliance training including the Verhaltenskodex and Datenschutz-Schulung. Each new hire is assigned an Onboarding Buddy from a different team for the first 90 days to provide informal guidance and cultural integration. A structured 30-60-90 day plan with milestone check-ins ensures alignment, with the Probezeit assessment conducted by the manager at the end of month five.

## Mental Health and Well-Being Programs
> **Markets:** Berlin, Remote | **Categories:** Well-Being, Benefits

Enpal provides confidential mental health support through our partnership with nilo.health, offering all employees and their dependents up to 12 free therapy sessions per year with licensed psychologists in German or English. The monthly Mental Health Day initiative allows employees to take one additional paid day off per quarter for mental well-being, no questions asked and no medical certificate required. Our internal Mental Health First Aiders network includes 15 trained volunteers across departments who serve as confidential first points of contact (Vertrauenspersonen). Berlin-based employees can access in-office mindfulness sessions every Wednesday, and remote employees receive a Calm app premium subscription through our benefits portal.

## Equal Opportunity and Anti-Discrimination
> **Markets:** Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich, Hamburg, Remote | **Categories:** Policies, Well-Being

Enpal is committed to fostering an inclusive workplace under the Allgemeines Gleichbehandlungsgesetz (AGG), prohibiting discrimination based on race, ethnic origin, gender, religion, disability, age, or sexual identity. Our diversity targets aim for at least 35% of leadership positions to be held by women by 2026, with progress reviewed quarterly by the executive team. Employees experiencing discrimination may report through our anonymous Whistleblower-Hotline operated by an external ombudsperson or directly to their HR business partner. All people managers complete mandatory Unconscious Bias Training within their first 90 days and biennially thereafter, along with AGG compliance refreshers.
